How to Generate Podcast Episodes

  1. Click a lesson in the sidebar
  2. Copy the Lesson Content text below
  3. Go to notebooklm.google.com and create a new notebook
  4. Click "Add source" → paste the lesson content as a text source
  5. Copy the Customization Instructions and paste them in the podcast customization field (click the podcast icon, then "Customize")
  6. Click Generate and wait (~3-5 min)
  7. Download the MP3 and save it to the path shown below

Select a lesson from the sidebar to get started.
