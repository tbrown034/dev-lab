Learn R

R is the language built for data and statistics — strong at analysis, modeling, and publication-quality charts. If you already know Python or SQL, you have most of the mental model; R just has its own dialect, plus the tidyverse (dplyr, ggplot2).

Lessons are being written. In the meantime, the AI tutor on this page is live — ask it anything about vectors, data frames, dplyr verbs, or ggplot2, and it will explain through Python, SQL, and JS analogies.
