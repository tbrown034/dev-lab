What You Can Build Now

After completing these 12 lessons, you can confidently build:

  • Bar charts, line charts, area charts, donut charts
  • Choropleth and interactive maps
  • Filterable, sortable, animated dashboards
  • Tooltips, hover effects, transitions
  • Responsive, accessible visualizations

For the interview: You understand D3's mental models, not just its API. You know that selections are DOM handles, scales are translators, projections are geographic scales, path generators are cookie cutters, and data binding attaches sticky notes. You can read any D3 code and explain what it does.
